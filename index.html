ABOUT US

At Save N Serve, we're on a mission to make a difference one meal at a time. We believe that no one should go to bed hungry, and no plate of food should go to waste. Our platform serves as a bridge connecting compassionate restaurants and dedicated NGOs, creating a synergy that feeds the hungry and reduces food wastage.

How it works?

Restaurants partner with us to share their excess cooked and raw food. Our network of NGOs then steps in to collect and distribute these meals to the less fortunate. Together, we are redefining food distribution, minimizing waste, and nourishing communities in need.

        Join Us

We invite you to be a part of our journey. Whether you're a restaurant looking to make a meaningful impact or an NGO committed to ensuring no one goes to bed hungry.

  Our Mission

Reduce Food Waste: We're dedicated to curbing the staggering problem of food waste.
Alleviate Hunger: Our platform aims to eradicate hunger one meal at a time by ensuring that perfectly good food reaches those who need it the most.
Build Partnerships: We foster collaboration between restaurants, NGOs, and volunteers.

Together, we're turning surplus into smiles - join us in the fight against hunger and food waste!

By bridging hearts and plates, we're turning surplus into sustenance, reducing food waste, and ensuring that no one goes to bed hungry. Together, we can make a difference in the fight against food waste and hunger, transforming compassion into nourishment.
